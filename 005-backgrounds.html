Backgrounds

There are several things that someone can do with backgrounds. The elements that can are usually added in the background of a page are:

  • A background color:
     div{background-color: green;} 
  • A background color with some transparency:
     div{background-color: rgba(0, 155, 255, .5);} 
  • An image:
     div{background-image: url("");} 
  • An image's position as a background:
     div{background-position: 100px 5px;}
  • An image's repeat as a background:
     div{background-repeat: none}
